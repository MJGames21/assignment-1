About Me

needed. I have several years of customer service experience which has helped me to
grow and learn many life skills and thrive in this environment. I provide fast and friendly
service. I use my organizational skills daily when I stock and organize shelves and
displays. I developed my conflict resolution and critical thinking skills when I spent time
volunteering at a daycare where I played and interact with children in the Infant room
and the playground. I use my communication skills when I assist other co-workers
when working in a team atmosphere and a an individual.
